How do we help!

We operate by evaluating your coding skills, either through challenges or project submissions. Using this information, we then match you with relevant internship opportunities. We assist you in creating application materials, such as customized resumes and cover letters, and provide interview preparation resources to ensure you're well-equipped for success. Our aim is to streamline the process of finding coding-related internships for engineering students.

How do we Work?

We begin by understanding your coding proficiency. When you sign up on our platform, you'll have the chance to showcase your coding skills through a range of challenges or by uploading your projects. This assessment helps us grasp your strengths and areas of expertise, enabling us to match you with the most suitable internship roles.

Based on your skill assessment, our intelligent algorithm generates a list of personalized internship recommendations. These recommendations align with your coding proficiency, ensuring that you're presented with opportunities that resonate with your capabilities and interests.

Our services are completely free for engineering students. We're committed to breaking down financial barriers and making internship opportunities accessible to all. Here we're more than just a platform; we're your partners in shaping a successful engineering career. Join us today and unlock a world of internship possibilities that truly align with your coding skills and aspirations.
